Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in 1998, Boccone began as a small family dream to bring the vibrant, robust flavors of our hometown in Tuscany to a new audience. What started with just ten tables has grown into a culinary landmark.

Our philosophy is simple: use only the freshest ingredients, prepare them with care, and serve them with genuine warmth. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the spirit of our heritage.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving the art of traditional Italian cooking while embracing modern culinary techniques. We source our produce locally whenever possible, and import our olive oils, cheeses, and select cured meats directly from artisan producers in Italy.

We believe dining is more than just eating—it is an experience to be shared, a moment to connect with family and friends around a table filled with love.
